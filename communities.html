Communities

Where Seawell is opening.

Seawell is building a thoughtful portfolio of senior living communities across California, with expansion planned into select Western states. Each location is chosen for its climate, community fabric, and access to quality healthcare — and each is purpose-built around our four commitments.

Our approach to real estate is deliberately unhurried. We would rather open fewer communities well than more communities quickly. Each Seawell property is purpose-designed in collaboration with gerontologists, environmental psychologists, interior designers, and — critically — operations leadership who have run senior living communities before. We build with the people who will work there and the people who will live there in mind, from the first schematic drawing.

How we choose locations

Selecting a site for a senior living community is a different exercise from choosing a site for a hotel or an apartment building. The variables that matter are specific to the people who will live there.

We look first at community fabric. Is this a place where a resident's adult children will actually visit? Are there restaurants, cultural institutions, and gathering places that residents can enjoy? Is the surrounding neighborhood welcoming to older adults rather than designed purely for younger, busier lives?

We look at healthcare access. Proximity to quality hospitals, specialty care, rehabilitation services, and a deep pool of home health and hospice providers. In senior living, a community's proximity to good healthcare matters every day, and matters especially in the moments that define care.

We look at climate. California's Mediterranean climate is genuinely good for older adults — it supports outdoor activity year-round, reduces seasonal mood and isolation issues common in harsher climates, and offers a long list of microclimates to match residents' preferences.

We look at workforce. The operational quality of a senior living community depends entirely on the staff we can hire and retain. We site communities in markets where we can access a deep pool of care professionals and offer them careers worth staying for.

Expansion beyond California

After establishing our California portfolio, Seawell plans expansion into select Western states — Arizona, Colorado, Washington, and Oregon are in early evaluation. Our expansion approach prioritizes quality over pace. We will open a next community when we can do so well, not when capital markets want us to.

Family interest

If you are researching senior living for a family member and one of the Seawell communities is in your consideration set, we would welcome a conversation — even a year or more before a potential move. Family planning for senior living benefits from time, and we are happy to be part of your research phase without any pressure to commit.
